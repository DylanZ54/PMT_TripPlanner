Discover Taiwan

Explore the most beautiful cities and destinations across Taiwan

🏙️ Taipei

The vibrant capital of Taiwan, known for its night markets, Taipei 101 skyscraper, and rich cultural heritage.

⚓ Kaohsiung

A vibrant harbor city famous for its art scene, night markets, and beautiful Cijin Island beaches.

🎨 Taichung

A perfect blend of modern urban vibes and natural beauty with parks, museums, and bustling night markets.

🏮 Tainan

Taiwan’s oldest city, rich in temples, history, and famous for its traditional street food and cultural festivals.

🌧️ Keelung

A northern port city famous for its rainy weather, Miaokou Night Market, and lively Ghost Festival.

⛰️ Hualien

Gateway to Taroko Gorge, Hualien offers dramatic mountain views, nature trails, and indigenous cultural experiences.

🚂 Chiayi

Starting point for Alishan adventures, Chiayi is known for turkey rice, forest railways, and Japanese-era architecture.

✈️ Taoyuan

An industrial and transport hub, Taoyuan features Hakka villages, cultural sites, and Taiwan’s main international airport.

🌄 Miaoli

A hilly, peaceful region rich in Hakka culture, known for wood sculpture art and scenic railway towns.

🏖️ Pingtung

Taiwan’s southern gem, home to Kenting National Park, stunning beaches, and vibrant indigenous cultures.
